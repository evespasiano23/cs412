About This Page

Hello! This page contains quotes and images of the famous Maya Angelou (1928-2014). You can click on her name to learn more information about her!


Maya Angelou was an American memoirist, civil rights activist, and essayist. She is famously known for her writing, primarily for her 1969 autobiography titled I Know Why the Caged Bird Sings. She is celebrated for her role as an advocate for women and Black culture.
